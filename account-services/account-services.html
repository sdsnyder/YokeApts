Account Services


Welcome to Yoke Apartments! Here in Account Services you will be able to create an account with us for your convenience. This account will help us track any discrepancies with your apartment. Once you create an account, you can easily login using your username and password.

Registration requirements

  • There are only 30 apartment units in this complex
  • First Names are limited to 28 letters, if you name exceeds 28 letters, use the first 28.
  • Last Names are limited to 28 letters, if you name exceeds 28 letters, use the first 28.
  • Usernames are limited to 28 letters, if you username exceeds 28 letters, use the first 28.
  • Passwords are limited to 12 letters.
