Services We Offer

Yoke Apartments offers variety of preventative, routine, and requested maintenance services to our residents. We know that needing maintenance work done in your apartment is not the ideal situation, so to make our residents' lives easier, we created an online maintenance request system. This system guarantees that residents receive a response to their requests within 24 hours.

Current residents should create and account on our website by going to the Account Services tab. After creating an account, residents can visit the Service Center tab to submit a maintenance request ticket. If you are not satisfied with how we handled your request, you may file a complaint by visiting the Complaints tab and your situation will be reviewed and addressed.

The services our maintenance technicians provide include:

  • Installing and repairing wires, plugs, and switches
  • Maintaining fire alarms, extinguishers, and emergency exits
  • Repairing apartment appliances
  • Oiling door knobs, locks, hinges, and gates
  • Replacing carpets and wooden floors
  • Repairing and unclogging toilets, sinks, bathtubs, showers, and exterior drains
  • Disposing of trash found in communcal areas
  • Unlocking apartments when keys are forgotten/lost by residents

Other services requested will be handled by maintence companies contracted out by Yoke Apartments.
